In 2025, life insurance has evolved far beyond its traditional role of providing death benefit protection. It has emerged as a sophisticated intergenerational financial tool that enables families to transfer wealth efficiently, preserve legacies, and create lasting financial security across multiple generations. This transformation represents one of the most significant developments in the insurance industry's history.

Modern intergenerational life insurance solutions combine advanced financial engineering with flexible policy structures to address complex family dynamics, tax considerations, and long-term wealth preservation goals.

How Wealth Moves Through Generations

Wealth Perpetuation

Permanent insurance creates lasting legacy that can benefit multiple future generations

Wealth Transfer Strategies and Mechanisms

Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts (ILIT)

Trust-owned policies that remove death benefits from taxable estates while providing controlled distributions to beneficiaries according to predetermined terms.

Family Limited Partnerships

Insurance policies integrated with FLP structures to leverage valuation discounts and maintain family control over asset distributions.

Dynasty Trust Funding

Permanent life insurance used to fund long-term trusts that can last for multiple generations beyond the initial grantor's lifetime.

Estate Equalization

Life insurance provides liquid assets to heirs who don't receive business interests or real estate, ensuring fair treatment among all children.
